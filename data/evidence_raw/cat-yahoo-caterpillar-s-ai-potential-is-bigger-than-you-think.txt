Artificial intelligence has its tentacles in many sectors of the economy beyond big tech, including heavy machinery.  JPMorgan analyst Tami Zakaria took a stab at sizing the opportunity in a Tuesday report.  “We estimate a potential for greater than $10 incremental earnings per share in the base case over the next three years, with capacity constraints the gating factor,” Zakaria wrote.
