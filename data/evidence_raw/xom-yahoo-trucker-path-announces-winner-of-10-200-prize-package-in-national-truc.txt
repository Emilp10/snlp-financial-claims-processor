source: Yahoo
date: 2025-10-14

Trucker Path, the most comprehensive and relied-upon mobile app for North American truckers, today announced that it has selected the winner of the fourth annual National Truck Driver Appreciation Sweepstakes (NTDAS) on behalf of its sponsor partners. The winner, chosen from entries submitted by drivers across the country, will receive over $10,000 in prizes intended to make life on the road more comfortable and rewarding.
