The US has approved several billion dollars' worth of Nvidia chip exports for American companies operating in the UAE. Meanwhile, Elon Musk's Neuralink Corp. which aims to implant chips in people with neurological issues and generate revenue has been trialed on patients. Gayatri Sarkar, CEO and Founder of venture capital firm, Advaita Capital whose portfolio includes Neuralink spoke to Bloomberg's Horizons Middle East & Africa anchor Joumanna Bercetche about its usage & success so far.
