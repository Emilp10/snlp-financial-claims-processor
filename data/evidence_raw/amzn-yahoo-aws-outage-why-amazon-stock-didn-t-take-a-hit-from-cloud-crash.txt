Amazon started off the week racing to fix a massive cloud outage that drove errors for users of apps from McDonald's to Robinhood Markets.  Despite the AWS meltdown, Amazon stock is poised to end the week with a modest gain.  The gains Monday came even as the extent of its Amazon Web Services outage became clear early that morning and set off cascading problems across the broader internet throughout the day.
