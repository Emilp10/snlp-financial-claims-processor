The Chevron El Segundo refinery explosion and fire last week was a wakeup call that the California Energy Commission (CEC) needs to mandate minimum refinery inventory and resupply rules ASAP, Consumer Watchdog wrote in comments to the CEC. While so far consumers have dodged a big increase in gas prices over the last week, the explosion shows how much the rules are needed.
