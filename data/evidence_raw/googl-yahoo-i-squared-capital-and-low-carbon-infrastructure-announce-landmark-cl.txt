DECATUR, Ill. & PALO ALTO, Calif., October 23, 2025--I Squared Capital, a leading global infrastructure investor, and its portfolio company Low Carbon Infrastructure (LCI) today announced a landmark clean energy partnership with Google to develop Broadwing Energy, an over 400-megawatt and over 1.5 million pounds of steam an hour natural gas-fired cogeneration power plant integrated with carbon capture and sequestration (CCS) technology.
