title: Americans Are Falling Behind on Their Car Payments
source: Yahoo
date: 2025-10-10

To afford an automobile, more consumers, especially lower-income families, have resorted to buying used cars and taking out longer loans.  Now, more are falling behind on their loans, signaling that lower-income consumers are struggling to afford payments as wages stagnate and unemployment ticks higher.  While the economy has remained strong, and Wall Street has kept buying subprime auto loans, the auto market is evidence that not all is well under the hood.
