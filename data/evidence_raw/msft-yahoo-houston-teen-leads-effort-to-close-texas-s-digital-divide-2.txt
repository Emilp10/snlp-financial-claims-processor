A Houston-area high school senior is helping bring technology and opportunity to students and communities that need it most. Rayaan Khan, a senior at Klein Collins High School in Spring, has taken a leading role in expanding 360Assist, the philanthropic arm of Works360, a global leader in technology enablement and demo-kit solutions. Texans can now nominate local organizations for future donation rounds at 360-assist.com/nominate .
