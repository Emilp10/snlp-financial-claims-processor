NEW YORK, October 15, 2025--CyViation, a cybersecurity company dedicated to aviation safety, is collaborating with Boeing Aviation Business Solutions (ABS) to support aircraft cyber risk assessments and compliance. ABS will begin offering CyViation’s SkyRay™ platform to support its clients with evidence-based solutions in their Digital and Cybersecurity consulting practice.
