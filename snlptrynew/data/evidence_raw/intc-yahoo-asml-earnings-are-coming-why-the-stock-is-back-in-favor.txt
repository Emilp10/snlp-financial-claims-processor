ASML Holding  will report its third-quarter earnings on Wednesday.  ASML is expected to report earnings of 5.45 euros ($6.30) a share, on revenue of €7.74 billion ($8.9 billion), according to a FactSet poll of analysts’ estimates.  The Dutch company’s American depositary receipts (ADRs) have been on an impressive run lately, surging more than 30% from their level at the start of September.
