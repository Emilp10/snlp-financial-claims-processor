OTTAWA, Ontario, Oct. 08, 2025 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- CCC is pleased to announce that it was awarded a contract by the United States Department of Defense (U.S. DoD) for magnetic amplifiers made by Anodyne Electronics Manufacturing Corp (AEM). The amplifiers will support the internal communication system of V-22 Osprey aircraft operated by the U.S. Navy.
