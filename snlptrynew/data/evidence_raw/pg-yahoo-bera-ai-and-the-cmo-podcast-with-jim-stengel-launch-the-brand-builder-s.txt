BERA.ai and The CMO Podcast with Jim Stengel today announced the launch of The Brand Builder's Playbook, an eight-part podcast designed to give marketers a data-driven guide to what actually works in brand building today. Jim Stengel, former Chief Marketing Officer of Procter & Gamble and author, and Ryan Barker, CEO of BERA.ai, co-host the podcast.
