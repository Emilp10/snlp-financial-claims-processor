STANTON, Tenn (Reuters) -Stanton, Tennessee - population 450 - welcomed a massive new neighbor a few years ago: a Ford electric-truck and joint venture battery plant slated to employ 6,000 workers.  Ford’s 2022 groundbreaking triggered an influx of construction activity into the former cotton-and-soybean farmlands outside of Memphis.  Stanton is quieter these days.
