BENTONVILLE, Ark., October 21, 2025--Walmart today announced the return of its beloved annual Thanksgiving meal, with its best offer yet. This incredible basket offers over 20 national and private brand items – including a Butterball turkey for $.97 per pound, its lowest price since 2019 – as a one-click basket serving 10 people for less than $40. It’s also offering free express delivery on the meal basket for first-time Pickup and Delivery customers.
