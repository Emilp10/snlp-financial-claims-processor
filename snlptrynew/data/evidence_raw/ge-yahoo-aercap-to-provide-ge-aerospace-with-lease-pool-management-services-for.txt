AerCap Holdings N.V. ("AerCap" or the "Company") (NYSE: AER) today announced it has signed a seven-year agreement with GE Aerospace (NYSE: GE) to provide lease pool management services for the GE9X engine. The agreement also extends AerCap's ongoing lease pool support for GEnx, GE90, CF6 and CF34 engines.
