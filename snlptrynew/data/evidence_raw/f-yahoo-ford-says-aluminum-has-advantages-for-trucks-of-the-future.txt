Ford Motor says it remains committed to aluminum, despite tight supplies after a major fire that halted production at a Novelis plant in New York for the rest of the year. The company was effectively pushing back at Lourenco Goncalves, the chief executive of Cleveland-Cliffs, who suggested on Monday that automakers were strongly considering switching aluminum designs back to steel.
