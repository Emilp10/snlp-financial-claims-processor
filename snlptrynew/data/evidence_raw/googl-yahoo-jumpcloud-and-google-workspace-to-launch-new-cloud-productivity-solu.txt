JumpCloud Inc. today announced it is collaborating with Google Workspace to deliver the Work Transformation Set. This solution combines secure, AI-powered productivity and collaboration tools from Google Workspace with JumpCloud's identity, device, and access management. This is offered to enterprises in a single, cost-effective price and contract from Google.
