Shares of  Amphenol  the maker of fiber-optic cables and connectors, have surged 81% this year.  BofA  Securities upgraded Amphenol to Buy from Neutral on Wednesday, raising its target for the stock price to $150 from $120.  Artificial intelligence is “driving interconnect demand even higher,” the bank said.
