Brevan Electronics, a leading global distributor of electronic components, today announced the acquisition of Micro Informatica, the largest authorized distributor of AMD products in Latin America. Established 39 years ago, Micro has built a reputation as a trusted leader in high-performance computing and semiconductor distribution across both Latin America and the United States.
