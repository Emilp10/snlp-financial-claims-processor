date: 2025-10-14

Oracle will put 50,000 of the semiconductors in data center computers starting in the third quarter of 2026, according to a statement Tuesday.  The systems will contain AMD processors and networking components.  The Santa Clara, California-based company is working to become a credible alternative to Nvidia, the dominant provider of AI processors.
