BOSTON, October 16, 2025--EMD Serono, the healthcare business of Merck KGaA, Darmstadt, Germany, in the U.S. and Canada, and President Donald J. Trump’s administration, today announced an agreement to expand access to the company’s portfolio of in vitro fertilization (IVF) therapies for the more than 10 million American women struggling to have a baby.
